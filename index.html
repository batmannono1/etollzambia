When you open a PREPAID eToll account, each vehicle in your fleet is issued an eToll Card, meaning drivers no longer have to carry cash to pay for Tolls at all Toll Plazas and Weighbridges within Zambia.

• “Tap and Go” cashless toll process at the Toll Plazas.

• Eliminates time-consuming toll expense reporting, reimbursements and/or petty cash deployment along with improved controls and monitoring systems within your organization
